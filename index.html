    Mission

    The mission of Appcode is to educate a new generation of ethical leaders in Africa; to cultivate within our students the critical thinking skills, the concern for others and the courage it will take to transform a continent. we aim to advance knowledge and educate students in the field of IT, and other areas of scholarship that will best serve the nation and the world in the 21st century and beyond.

    We are committed to generating, disseminating, and preserving knowledge, and to working with others to bring this knowledge to bear on the world’s great challenges. Appcode is dedicated to providing its students with an education that combines rigorous academic study and the excitement of discovery with the support and intellectual stimulation of a diverse campus community. We seek to develop in each member of the Appcode community the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively, and effectively for the betterment of humankind.

    Vision

    To make the world our campus through industry partnerships and experiential learning by teaching the students of today the skills of tomorrow forever.

    Shared Values

    Forward-thinking: We strive to be the leaders of change. We anticipate hurdles, innovate, and make incremental improvements by embracing an innovative and proactive attitude.

    Forward-thinking: We adopt an equity framework that proactively seeks to make Appcode accessible, affordable, and inclusive for all people.
